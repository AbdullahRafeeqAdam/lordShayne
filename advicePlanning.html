Advice & Planning

Whether you're a first generation entrepreneur, or a family working hard to preserve your generational wealth, understanding your goals and having an effective financial plan in place is vital.

From the design to the implementation of your wealth strategies

We are convinced of the benefits of a long-lasting relationship based on mutual trust and mutual knowledge, in order to serve our clients' best interests. We take into account the specificities and complexities of your situation in order to provide tailored advice and establish a global strategy. Our private bankers, wealth engineers and portfolio managers work in close collaboration to provide you with support and daily monitoring of your situation and objectives.

Wealth solutions

Our wealth engineers are distinguished by their technical expertise, responsiveness, and proactive approach in anticipating your unique needs. Working closely with your private banker and portfolio manager, they craft tailored solutions for both tax and legal matters.

They offer guidance on structuring your professional assets in collaboration with our Corporate Advisory Services teams. Additionally, they help organize your personal assets by establishing holding structures customized to your specific situation and objectives. Succession planning is also meticulously handled within a controlled tax framework, supported by our Family Office services.

Our wealth engineers are actively engaged at every critical stage of your personal life (family changes, succession planning, changes in tax residence) or professional life (company sale, retirement, etc.).

Our Family Office

Our Family Office assists you on a daily basis to meet all your needs in terms of wealth structuring, asset management and monitoring, financing, unlisted assets, real estate, art, philanthropy, governance and special secretarial services.
