About Us

Open Ocean Capital is a leading global financial services group with a values-driven culture.

Having been at the centre of the world's financial markets for over 200 years, our expertise, intellectual capital and global network enable us to provide a distinct perspective that makes a meaningful difference to our clients, communities and planet.

Our businesses

The way we do business reflects both our long-term commitment to our clients and the guiding principles of our group.

Our focus is – and has been for over 200 years – on long-term growth. As a family-controlled business we are unconstrained by short-term thinking and take a long-term view. In this way, our business model is stable and sustainable.

Our group consists of four established and complementary businesses:

Corporate Sustainability

At Open Ocean Capital, we have the ambition to use our influence and expertise to support the sustainability transition of the global economy.

Our Global Scale

Thanks to a broad network of over 60 offices in more than 40 countries, we are uniquely placed to combine unrivalled in-depth local knowledge with global capabilities for the benefit of our clients worldwide.

Our People

We understand that our business is built on the strength of our people and our relationships. We employ talented and motivated graduates and professionals, and nurture a dynamic and collaborative culture where everyone feels included and respected. Our teams reflect the diverse needs of our clients, businesses and the markets we operate in around the world.

The long-term outlook that our clients have come to value and expect from us also extends to our colleagues . We work hard to develop our people so that they can build fulfilling careers, and our clients also welcome the continuity in our service.

Our heritage and guiding principles

❝ Our heritage provides us with both a strong foundation and a clear vision for the future ❞

Example Name - Executive Chairman

We are a values-driven organization, built on seven generations of family leadership and a 200-year legacy.

Our premier financial services are grounded in our dedication to excellence, our client-focused approach, and our long-term perspective. These values are reinforced by our core principles: Thoughtful, Creative, and Principled, inspired by the Open Ocean Capital motto Concordia, Integritas, Industria (harmony, integrity, industry).

These principles also shape our commitment to best practices and ongoing improvements in ESG considerations. We foster a culture of responsible business where our people are accountable for their work, actions, and the impact they have on our clients, our industry, the environment, and our communities.

Thoughtful

Clients trust that our perspectives are rooted in our deep experience. Our talented teams build on this foundation of experience and objectively research and tailor every solution to serve our clients' needs. The advice we give and the solutions we develop are considered, strategic, and focused on the long-term.

Principled

We treat our clients' challenges with the same care and responsibility that we apply to our own business and money. We see integrity and empathy as the basis of long-term trusted relationships and a progressive business. This is reflected in an extraordinary personal commitment to our clients' challenges and consistently high execution standards in everything we do.

Creative

200 years of legacy in entrepreneurship and the promotion of imaginative solutions for our clients means we have always been open to new ideas. Our depth of insight and track record give us the confidence to embrace innovative new solutions for our clients to jointly solve their individual challenges every day.

A business spanning four centuries

From the late 18th century to the present day we have remained at the centre of the world's financial markets.
