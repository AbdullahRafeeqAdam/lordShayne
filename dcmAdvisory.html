As an independent advisor, we guide clients through the entire IPO process, offering objective and informed advice on timing, structural choices, bank appointments, investor targeting, roadshows, deal strategies, and share allocations. We coordinate all involved parties to design the optimal transaction and collaborate with major listing agencies to ensure a successful listing, no matter where our clients are located.

For currently listed companies we provide this same value add when helping our clients deliver further equity raisings or disposals.

Our services in Listed Equity include:

> IPOs

> Multi-track IPO and M&A exit processes

> Secondary offerings

> Capital raisings, including rights issues and placings

> Block trades and sell-downs

> Spin-offs and demergers

> Equity linked issues, such as convertible or exchangeable bonds

> SPACs, deSPACs and SPAC mergers
