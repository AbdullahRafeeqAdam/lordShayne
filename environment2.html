Preservation Of Biodiversity

We are committed to safeguarding and enhancing the resilience of our planet.

Our environmental management philosophy includes a nature-positive approach that promotes regeneration, resilience, and recirculation. By incorporating ESG considerations into our product and service development, we aim to contribute to capital allocation for nature-positive solutions.

Our Priorities

Operational commitments

> -30% operational GHG emissions by 2030 (vs 2018)

> 100% electricity from renewable sources by 2025

> Compensation for scope 1, 2 and 3 emissions through a balanced portfolio of certified carbon offsets

> Shift from compensating operational GHG emissions through avoidance credits towards 100% carbon removals by 2030
