Charities

Philanthropy has always been a key preoccupation of our founding family.

Open Ocean Capital’s Commitment

A direct legacy of a family tradition spanning centuries, philanthropy is deeply ingrained in Open Ocean Capital’s culture. We have established the OOC Generations platform to unite initiatives across our global locations, supporting social and environmental causes through local and international projects. Our employees also demonstrate a strong commitment to these values within the company.

A dedicated team

Throughout its history, Open Ocean Capital has developed specific expertise in non-profit organisations, including associations, foundations, endowment funds, professional federations, religious associations etc. This group of experts, who are often themselves personally involved in the life of associations, advises and manages their clients' assets in order to expand and perpetuate their resources.

We support you in making your commitment a reality

Our teams of private bankers and wealth engineers excel in integrating philanthropic projects into clients' wealth strategies, considering legal structures, financial engineering, and tax schemes tailored to their objectives and family situations.

In collaboration with various teams across the Private Bank, the OOC Generations foundation offers pre-structuring support to clients, guiding them through the exploration of possibilities—motivations, strategies, causes to support, methods of assistance, and timelines. We also provide tools for monitoring progress, including recommendations of relevant organizations, project visits, data collection, and impact measurement.

Your challenges

Foundations and associations often stem from the private initiatives of individuals, families, or groups who are motivated to combat illness, disability, or any other affront to human dignity. These initiatives may also be launched to honor the legacy of a loved one or to build a project in their memory. Additionally, many actions are carried out in the artistic and cultural spheres. The younger generations display a strong desire to contribute and give back through donations. They tend to adopt a critical and professional approach to their philanthropic activities, focusing particularly on the management of their charitable efforts.

Our advice

Non-profit organizations require asset management that often differs significantly from that of individual clients, primarily because these organizations outlast their founders. With a very long-term investment horizon, their focus is more on capital preservation than on seizing stock market opportunities.

We recognize the unique challenges you face in balancing your charity's immediate needs with its future obligations. As your trusted advisor, we are here to help you plan for and achieve your organization's long-term goals, ensuring a positive impact for generations to come.
