Our Approach

Understanding our clients is key

Fostering trusted relationships with our clients has been central to Open Ocean Capital’s philosophy for over 200 years. The effectiveness of this approach is demonstrated by substantial repeat business and long-term partnerships spanning decades.

Global scale

We emphasize the value of local market expertise and being present to support our clients, no matter their location or the nature of their business. We then leverage this insight across our network to develop exceptional sector and market knowledge. With teams in over 40 countries, we provide comprehensive market coverage.

Trusted by every type of client

Our sector experts encompass every industry, advising clients of all sizes—from raising capital for growth companies to providing mid-market expertise and serving many of the world's largest multinational corporations. We support the mid-market through specialized teams in the UK (Arrowpoint Advisory) and in France and Brazil.

We possess unparalleled experience in advising financial sponsors and comprehending their objectives. We are the preferred advisor for sovereigns and state-owned enterprises. With our own family-controlled background and a robust entrepreneurial spirit, we uniquely understand the challenges faced by family and founder shareholders and investors.
